**DEPARTMENT FOR WORK AND PENSIONS**

**Travel and Subsistence Policy**

Version 2.0

Date: April 2021

Owner: People Services

1\. Introduction

This document sets out the Department’s policy on travel and subsistence. All staff must comply with this policy when travelling on official business.

2\. Travel

2.1 Rail

Standard class is the default. First class may be used where the journey is over 3 hours and the traveller needs to work during the journey.

2.2 Air

Flights within the UK mainland are not normally approved. Economy class for all international travel.

2.3 Car

Mileage rates:

|             |                                             |
|-------------|---------------------------------------------|
| **Vehicle** | **Rate**                                    |
| Car         | 45p per mile (first 10,000), 25p thereafter |
| Motorcycle  | 24p per mile                                |

3\. Subsistence Rates

|                  |                      |
|------------------|----------------------|
| **Item**         | **Amount**           |
| Breakfast        | £4.50                |
| Lunch            | £4.50                |
| Evening meal     | £12.50               |
| London overnight | up to £120 per night |
| Elsewhere        | up to £75 per night  |

4\. Claims

Claims must be submitted within 2 months. Receipts required for items over £10.

5\. Approvals

All claims approved by line manager.

Next review: April 2022
